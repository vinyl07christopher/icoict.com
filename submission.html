Submission Guideline

Template


Complete submissions should be no more than eight pages (including figures, tables, and bibliography) in a standard IEEE two-column style; articles that exceed the page restriction will be rejected without review.

Submission

Submit the paper via icicit.conference@gmail.com All the submitted articles with be peer-reviewed by members of the International Technical Committee

Presentation


As per ICICIT 2024, accepted articles must be delivered at the conference online or offline, by one of the authors in accordance with the announced schedule. Authors will have 20 minutes to present their work.



Authors Guidelines

Prospective writers are invited to submit complete technical papers of their work electronically. The conference's working language is English.

Authors must explicitly explain the work's relevance, emphasize novel features, and characterize its present orientation.

If you need to change any author information or resubmit your article, you can do so until the submission deadline.

Accepted and presented papers will be copyrighted to IEEE and published in the proceedings of the conference, which will be accepted for inclusion in the IEEE XploreÂ® Digital Library once an IEEE quality review is completed.

Only original content, not previously published or copyrighted, should be submitted. Prospective authors must submit only their own unique content.

The CrossCheck automatic screening method will be used by the Conference to assist in checking the originality of papers. Authors will be notified of article acceptance or rejection by email as possible to the published author notification date.

Submissions must be made by the deadline. No exceptions will be made.

Final Submission: The author must provide the final version of the manuscript(s) and electronically sign the "IEEE Copyright Form" before the final submission date.

( Note: Final manuscript should be updated on the comments provided by the technical team)

Reminder: Any paper accepted for the technical program but not presented will have its official proceedings removed from IEEE Xplore.
